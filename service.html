OUR CORE BUSINESS
It is about finding, Raise (Allocating) funds to produce and invest on major
oil infrastructures (Upstream- Midle-and Downstream) marketing the
natural resources (Crude Oil) on which the modern world depends. The
Group’s activities include. Assist on production of crude oil and its
derivative; refining, marketing, supply and transportation, and the
manufacture of petrochemicals.
We are working in partnership with a major diversified group of energy &
petrochemical companies.
We are capable to develop Midstream main infrastructures such as
building Oil Refineries and Tank Farms with minimum processing capacity
of 200.000BBL Per day and above with construction period of 3 years.
The refinery construction its seen as complement to our trading function
by creating greater operational efficiency across the supply chain
